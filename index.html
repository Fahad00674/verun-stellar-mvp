How it works

Three modes. One protocol.

01 · Discovery
Agent finds the platform
Agent scans the verified platform registry, filters by MiFID II + AI Act audit trail, identifies optimal match. No human input required.
02 · Supervised
Human reviews and approves
Structured referral with platform score, risk tier, proposed action. One-click approve or deny. Audit trail anchored on Stellar.
03 · Autonomous
Agent runs fully automated
Within policy bounds set by human. Revocable. Continuous monitoring. Every transaction logged on-chain. AI Act Art. 14 compliant by design.
